About REFRAME Studio

REFRAME Studio is a collection of ultra-simple browser tools designed to reduce small everyday thinking friction.

What this project is

Many tools today are powerful but complicated. REFRAME Studio explores the opposite idea: tiny tools that solve one small problem instantly.

No login. No tracking. No AI APIs. Everything runs entirely in your browser.

Why it exists

Modern thinking is fragmented across many tools, AI chats, notes, and drafts.

REFRAME Studio aims to create small thinking utilities that help people clarify ideas, generate content, and reduce mental friction.

Philosophy

Small tools. Clear thinking. Private by default.
